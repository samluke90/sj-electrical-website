About SJ Electrical

Your trusted local electricians in East London

Trusted Electrical Experts Since Day One

SJ Electrical Contractors Limited was founded with a simple mission: to provide East London homes and businesses with reliable, high-quality electrical services at fair prices.

Based in the E4 area, we've built our reputation on honest work, transparent pricing, and a commitment to safety that never wavers. Every job, whether it's replacing a single socket or rewiring an entire property, receives our full attention and expertise.

As NICEIC registered contractors, we meet the highest industry standards. Our work is regularly assessed to ensure compliance with all electrical regulations, giving you peace of mind that your electrics are in safe hands.
